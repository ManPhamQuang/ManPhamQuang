Interest in IT

When did your interest in IT start?

Machine learning, UI/UX designs, algorithms, video games, ... I'm generally interested in a lot of fields in IT since I was a little boy.

It was not until the first bash script that I wrote (or copied from the internet) to crack a video game that actually got my hands dirty configuring source codes and variable files.

I started learning to do actual programming out of curiosity after playing a puzzle level in a video game when i was a child. Starting out with html and css for basic websites, I quickly learned javascript and adapted the fullstack javascript trend.

Having dipped my toe in building web sites and phone apps a couple of times, I am now looking forward to study various algebra at RMIT to be able to do machine learning

Why did you choose to come to RMIT?

RMIT is one of the university in Vietnam well known for international programmes, it is only natural for me to choose to enroll here. Having said that, there are some benefits at RMIT that other university simply do not offer:

  • Free Student Services
  • Up to date facilities
  • International exchange programs
  • Campus located in Ho Chi Minh city, one of the most economically/socially active city in Vietnam

What do you expect to learn during your studies?

I have very high expectation studying at RMIT, the following are my anticipation:

  • Problem solving/critical thinking skills
  • Algorithms in depth
  • Technology in general, to be able to understand and adapt most fields
  • Soft skills such as work ethic, management skills, teamwork, professionalism, etc..
